<objective>
Split InMemoryBridge into two single-purpose test doubles: InMemoryBridge (stateful, snapshot-based) and StubBridge (canned responses, no state).

Purpose: UAT Test 2 found that InMemoryBridge has two hidden modes (stateful vs stub) with auto-detection via seed data shape. This conflates two distinct responsibilities. Splitting them makes each class self-documenting and eliminates the _stateful flag and auto-detection heuristic.

Output: StubBridge class in its own module, InMemoryBridge cleaned of dual-mode logic, all stub-mode usages migrated.
</objective>
