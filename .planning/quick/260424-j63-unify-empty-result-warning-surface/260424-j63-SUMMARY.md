## Alphabetical Ordering Lock

Case 5 of the matrix is the canonical regression guard:

```python
# Active: due='today', estimated_minutes_max=30, flagged=True, parent='Work'
# Expected alphabetical camelCase order: due, estimatedMinutesMax, flagged, parent

    "The combination of filters 'due', 'estimatedMinutesMax', 'flagged', "
```
