This plan populates the enum values at the repo layer directly to keep the cross-path contract checkable — the service layer will either pass the assembled enum through unchanged or re-derive from the underlying booleans on the Task/Project rows; 56-03 locks the exact placement.

Until 56-03 moves the assembly, this plan computes the enum value *in the repo mapping function* from the two booleans (temporary, intentional — 56-03 relocates it to the service layer and removes the repo-level computation if that's the final placement).
