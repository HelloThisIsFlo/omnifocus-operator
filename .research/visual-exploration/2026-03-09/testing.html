Safety Net

Two runtime guards ensure tests never touch real OmniFocus data.

Bottom line: The automated suite proves that all logic — validation, caching, IPC, protocol, mapping — works correctly. The one thing it can't prove is whether OmniFocus itself behaves the way the code assumes. That's an inherently manual question, and that's what UAT is for.
